01. CRYPTOCURRENCIES

Introductory
Video

A cryptocurrency is a digital asset designed to work as a medium of exchange that uses cryptography to secure its transactions, to control the creation of additional units, and to verify the transfer of assets. Cryptocurrencies are a form of digital currencies, alternative currencies and virtual currencies.
